Allah Hears Every Whisper of Your Heart

Assalamu Alaikum,

In the quiet moments of the night, when the world slows down, our hearts often turn to Allah.

These final nights of Ramadan are a gift. A time when prayers are answered, sins are forgiven, and acts of charity are multiplied beyond measure.

Allah hears every whisper of your heart. Every worry you carry. Every hope you raise in du'a.

The Prophet ﷺ reminded us that in these blessed nights, seeking Allah's mercy through sincere worship and charity can bring immense reward.

Your Zakat and Sadaqah can be the relief a struggling family is praying for tonight.

● A meal for the hungry.

● Clean water for a village.

● Hope for a child in hardship.

This Ramadan, your generosity can change lives.

Give with sincerity. Give with certainty that Allah sees every act of kindness.
